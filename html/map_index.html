What this Map Shows:

We've got a new tool to focus outreach. This map shows all the areas of the city with a greater than 50% Black population. The darker the areas are, the lower the vaccination rate. There are some areas which the data is unavailable for, specifically portions in the central northern region of the lower 9th. However, this should give us an idea where we want to look at having in-person events in the city to draw the highest number of unvaccinated people.
